About This Website

Agriculture plays a major role in India's economic growth. Therefore predicting crop yield before its cultivation can help farmers and the agriculture departments in deciding which crop is better to grow and take appropriate marketing and storage measures. This Website focuses on predicting crop yield based on available data using Random Forest Machine Learning Algorithm and also give best recommendation on the type of Climate and soil in your Area.

Datasets used

The data used in the study was obtained from the website of the agricultural government portal. It contains state-wise, district-wise, crop-wise, season-wise, and year-wise data on crop covered area and production of crop yield in India.

Codes and Notebooks

All data preprocessing and analysis are done using the Google Colab short for Collaboratory.Colab is a Jupyter notebook that works in a browser using Google Cloud. You can Find the Jupyter Notebook in my Github Repo

Based Research

In this Research, the focus of predicting and the calculating yield was done with the help of Several Regression (machine learning) techniques. Out of which we found that the Random Forest was the best model to predict the crop yield
